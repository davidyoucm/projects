The Ultimate Serum for Dry Skin!

20 July 2020 | 0 Comments

Disclaimer: This post is NOT sponsored and we are in no way affiliated to Irén. However, this product was sent to me by Irén (thank you Irén!). As this product works wonders on my skin, I’m writing this review to share with you guys!! :’)

Hello guys!!! Today I’ve an amazing product to share with you guys!!! So recently, I spotted an Instagram post featuring 0.8L. Seeing that 0.8L allows users to try and review products from all over the world for free, I decided to give it a shot! The TRY FREE CAMPAIGN by 0.8L allows users to try products for free (note that users are selected on a random basis). All you have to do is share the reviews/campaigns on your social media site. 0.8L Singapore also has TRY-BUY NOW ($3.99 being the lowest price) which starts for TRY NOW & BUY NOW. The TRY NOW service is first come first service and when the TRY NOW service supplies ends, users get the chance to buy products at a price lower than retail price, depending on the amount of stocks there are. I was attracted by the very fact that they had ‘Try Free’ for the brand 3CE! (if you don’t already know, I recently splurged on many 3ce products and I absolutely love 3CE!) The idea of trying new products and being able to share my experience with others was just so tempting that I signed up with 0.8L. I did not get selected to try the 3CE Products. Just for fun, I decided to try applying for other products. Boy, I was shocked! I actually got selected to review the IRÉN SERUM.

So what is IRÉN?

According to Irén’s website, Irén's skincare products are created to fit the lifestyles of busy women. Working in synergy with a national research agency, Irén's unique patent formulations have gone through over 10,000 hours of clinical research and testing to provide time saving and proven results for your skin.

I was spoilt for choice! There are 7 customised serums, each made for different purposes - anti-aging, clearing, whitening, soothing, hydrating, anti-acne and anti-wrinkle. If you have other skin concerns, you may get the other serums :-) They cost $58 for 10ml. As mentioned, I have extremely dry skin, especially around my forehead, nose and lip area. Often, my skin is flaking and whenever I apply make up (which is rarely ever, but sometimes), my make up appears patchy and uneven; I always need to apply moisturiser in advance. As such, I picked the hydrating serum, which is highly suitable for people with dehydrated and rough skin. Then.. it came in the mailbox with a mini greeting card (thank you for being so thoughtful IRÉN!) and an informative leaflet.

According to IRÉN, this hydrating serum is made in Japan, with its ziplock encapsulation technology being patented. It is also 100% Vegan and cruelty free. Additionally, it has a maximum dosage of active ingredients (4% NMF Solution, Sodium Hyaluronate, Blueberry Extract, Betaine)!

Benefits:
  • Nourish dry skin
  • Promote skin radiance
  • Strengthen skin barrier with moisture
Ingredients: Aqua, Butylene Glycol, Glycerin, Betaine, Dipropylene Glycol, PEG-40 Hydrogenated Castor Oil, Sodium Hyaluronate, Vaccinium Angustifolium Fruit Extract, Sodium PCA, Sodium Lactate, Trehalose, Allantoin, Fructose, Glucose, Urea, Maltose, 1,2-Hexanediol, Pentylene Glycol, Hydroxyethylcellulose, Citric Acid, Ethylhexylglycerin, Xanthan Gum, Sodium Chloride, Sodium Citrate, Leuconostoc / Radish Root Ferment Filtrate, Sodium Hydroxide, Polyvinyl Alcohol, Phenoxyethanol, Chlorphenesin So of course, now what you’ve been waiting for... my personal experience. 

I was very confused on how to use the product at first because I failed to read the informative leaflet carefully (I was just too excited to try it out). As such, I had to ‘figure it out’ myself.

To be frank.. the product was so cute!!!!!! It reminded me of those days we used test drops during Chemistry & Biology classes lol. So anyways, apparently you just have to unscrew the lid and there’ll be this button thing at the top. Press it and it’ll suck the serum upwards, and it’ll be just enough for your face. It was advised to use it after their skin clear lotion, which obviously I didn’t have so I used it by itself. As adviced, I used 2-3 drops of serum (the amount you suck up once is just enough) and spread it all over my face. It feels rather light weight and doesn’t feel greasy at all; although I would say that i could still feel that I applied the serum on. After use, I felt that my skin appeared glowy and brighter. The next morning, my skin felt super moisturised and smooth!! :) I love how it’s possible to mix the serums together to customise it according to your skin needs! The only downside about this product is that it is pricey for its small size ($58 for 10ml).

Nevertheless, I would still highly recommend this product to anyone and everyone! Thank you IRÉN and 0.8L SG for sending this amazing hydrating serum to me! ☺️ With that, till my next review x
