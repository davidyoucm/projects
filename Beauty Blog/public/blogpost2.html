This Lipstick Collection Was My Best Buy of 2020!

1 August 2020 | 0 Comments

Disclaimer: This post is NOT sponsored and I am in no way affiliated to 3CE. Nevertheless, I paid $3.99 for this lipstick as 0.8L Singapore is currently having a TRY-BUY NOW campaign which enables users to buy products at a price lower than the retail price. You may check out how the TRY-BUY NOW campaign works here.

Recently, I've been into getting lipsticks that belong to the category: my-lips-but-better. In particular, a popular make up brand known as 3 Concept Eyes ("3CE") under the Korean fashion label StyleNanda, had caught my attention. I have personally bought a few myself from YesStyle and I found the range of lip shades offered by 3CE particularly suitable for daily wear. Additionally, as someone who doesn't really put make up, I noticed that korean make up products tend to be more suitable for users like me who prefer light make up, as compared to western ones.

Of course, being a sucker for 3CE products, I did not hesitate to get the lipstick in shade #116 (Inked Heart) when I saw that there was an ongoing TRY-BUY NOW Campaign for 3CE's Mood Recipe Lipstick. 3CE's Mood Recipe Lipstick #116 belongs to the Mood Recipe collection. Now, let me give you an overview of the 3CE Mood Recipe Lip Colour Collection first.

The Mood Recipe Lip Colour Collection contains 5 lip shades, of which the description for the various shades can be found below.

#114 ROWS: A nude beige tone befitting of runway models
#115 MUSS: Rose beige color is the ultimate statement of feminity with a softness that will make your face radiate.
#116 INKED HEART: A captivating peach-brown lipstick tone
#117 CHICFUL: A unique deep reddish brown lip color never is seen before
#909 SMOKED ROSE: On the borderline between ren and rose. Stylish shade that will instantly leave you feeling like a fashionista.

Next, I will be sharing more about the lip product sent to me.

3CE Inked Heart #116 is a light brownish orange MLBB shade, as you can see from the swatch below. This lipstick retails for $34.41 in Sephora Singapore (you may also get it from YesStyle!). When applied, the lip colour looks really natural on me!! :) It has a creamy matte finish. I absolutely love how pigmented inked heart is! It is also comfortable on my lips.

However, as with other lip products in the 3ce mood recipe collection, the downside of this product is that it’s not very long-lasting (not transfer proof/kiss proof, has to be re-applied throughout the day). When applied on dry lips, the product also doesn’t glide on v smoothly. Nevertheless, applying lip balm would do the trick!!! :-)

If I were to rate this lipstick according to the following criteria:

Blendability: 4.5/5.0
Staying power: 3.5/5
Colour payoff: 4.5/5

Overall, I’m a HUGE FAN of the 3ce Mood Recipe Collection. I would highly recommend this product to people looking for a natural, unique shade that is easy to pull off!!!!!☺️ I can’t wait to get my hands on more ‘TRY NOW’ 3CE products keke.
