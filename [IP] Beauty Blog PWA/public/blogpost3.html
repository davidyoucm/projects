As we grow older, it is only inevitable that we (girls being girls) start becoming more self-conscious about our looks. As such, many of us will start putting on make up. For many, make up is indeed the perfect confident booster, whether it is due to peer pressure or to "conceal" our flaws like pimples and blemishes. As long as you apply make up, the make up removal has to be part of your daily skincare routine. This is because cosmetic products can clog your pores and lead to irritation of your skin. Using make up removal reduces the risk of getting pimples, blackheads and blemishes. Of course, I am not an expert in this area. But everybody knows: getting a good make up removal is - no doubt - essential.
