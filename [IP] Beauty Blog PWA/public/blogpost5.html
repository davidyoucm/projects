  • experience difficulty focusing due to severe eyestrain?

As you might already know, Japan is so advanced in its technology that its products always never fail to beat those of its counterparts. Today, I'll be sharing about a some of the eyedrops that I purchased. I bought these eyedrops during my trip to Japan. During that trip, I had purchased quite a lot of beauty, skincare and health products. This eyedrop was recommended to me by my mother (who gave me a to-buy list), and is also highly raved on several online platforms!. If you are travelling to Japan and you are thinking of what kind of eyedrop to purchase, I would highly recommend these eyedrops to you!

    In the age of advanced information technology there is an increasing number of environments in which the eyes can become overworked. Such environments lead to declined focus-adjustment function and tired eyes (eye fatigue), manifesting as a heavy feeling in the back of the eyes. Sante Medical 12 is an eye drop for treating eye fatigue that contains a precise balance of twelve active ingredients, four of which are at the maximum concentrations,* including vitamin B12 and neostigmine methyl sulfate, which are effective for eye fatigue. Sante Medical 12 improves declined focus-adjustment function by working on the muscles and parasympathetic nerves responsible for adjusting focus, moisturizes dry eyes, and facilitates tissue metabolism by supplying nutrients to the eyes. This product supports a “healthy eye” life for people suffering eye strain due to the demands of modern society. Dosage: Instill 1 to 3 drops at a time, 5 to 6 times daily.

    Overall, I personally feel that this eyedrop is effective in relieving eye fatigue when there is too much stress is placed on the eyes. By facilitating tissue metabolism in tired eyes, it provides effective relief from symptoms such as tired eyes and red eyes. In addition, these eye drops cause a strong refreshing and invigorating sensation to spread from the surface of the eyes. If you want to refresh your eyes, this product will certainly meet you need! However, one must take note that the minty, burning feeling does take some time to get used to.
